Hyperandrogenism !! :: A condition of high levels of androgens (such as testosterone) in AFAB individuals. Symptoms may include acne, seborrhea (inflamed skin), hair loss on the scalp, increased body or facial hair, infrequent, irregular, or absent menstruation, and high blood cholesterol. Hyperandrogenism is not a diagnosis on its own, but is a symptom that occurs in several medical conditions including several conditions of intersex.
