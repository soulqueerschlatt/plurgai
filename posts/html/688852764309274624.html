Duosian !! :: A gender or sex for Intersex people.  A person with intersex conditions where there is a mix of male and female characteristics.   A person who has both “male” and “female” sexual characteristics, usually a penis and some “female” traits.

A non-cultural specific word for Androgynos, based off of Duogender (  Feeling that one’s gender is a combination of male and female as a result of being intersex.)
