Queer Attraction !! :: A form of attraction (like romantic, sexual, alterous, etc) that has no concrete definition by design, and is simply queer-in-nature.

Terms:

  • Qwish, or Quish, is the crush equivalent. [eg. I have a qwish on them.]
  • Moonfriend is a possible partner term. [eg. This is my moonfriend.]
  • Queer Partnership and Celestial Relationship are possible terms for the partnership. [eg. I’m in a queer partnership/celestial relationship with them.]

It can be a form of wavership as well, or just an attraction that cannot be defined by anything but Queer.
