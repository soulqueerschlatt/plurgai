Coining some First and Second person Pronouns

First Person:

(I/Me/My/Mine/Myself)

Example: Inside my soul, I don’t conform; can’t conform. They would all like to kill the non-conforming me. Which is me myself.

Ai/Mi/Mei/Min/Meiself

Example: Inside mei soul, ai don’t conform: can’t conform. They would all like to kill the non-conforming mi. Which is mi meiself. 

Hi/Hee/Hy/Hine/Hyself

Example: Inside hy soul, hi don’t conform: can’t conform. They would all like to kill the non-conforming hee. Which is hee hyself.

Shi/Shee/Shy/Shine/Shyself

Example: Inside shy soul, shi don’t conform: can’t conform. They would all like to kill the non-conforming shee. Which is shee shyself.

Zai/Zi/Zy/Zine/Zyself

Example: Inside zy soul, zai don’t conform: can’t conform. They would all like to kill the non-conforming zi. Which is zi zyself.

Second Person:

(You/You/Your/Yours/Yourself)

Example: What you do is, you fake a stomach cramp, and when you’re bent over, moaning and wailing, you lick your palms. It’s a little childish and stupid, but then, so is high school.

Y&/Y&/Yr&/Yrs&/Yrself&

Example: What y& do is, y& fake a stomach cramp, and when y&’re bent over, moaning and wailing, y& lick yr& palms. It’s a little childish and stupid, but then, so is high school.

O/O/Or/Ors/Orself

Example: What o do is, o fake a stomach cramp, and when o’re bent over, moaning and wailing, o lick or palms. It’s a little childish and stupid, but then, so is high school.

E/E/Er/Ers/Erself

Example: What e do is, e fake a stomach cramp, and when e’re bent over, moaning and wailing, e lick er palms. It’s a little childish and stupid, but then, so is high school.

Fe/Fe/Fer/Fers/Ferself

Example: What fe do is, fe fake a stomach cramp, and when fe’re bent over, moaning and wailing, fe lick fer palms. It’s a little childish and stupid, but then, so is high school.
