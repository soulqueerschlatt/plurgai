Androgynos (אַנְדְּרוֹגִינוֹס) !! ::  A Jewish cultural gender or sex for intersex people. This flag is specifically for Intersex people. A person who has both “male” and “female” sexual characteristics. In the Talmud, the androgynos is understood as someone who both has a penis as well as some female sex traits. 149 references in Mishna and Talmud; 350 in classical midrash and Jewish law codes.
