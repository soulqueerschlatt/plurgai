Plursex / Systemsex !! :: An umbrella term for members of a system with atypical sex characteristics. All sex characteristics count under this umbrella.

A Couple Examples ;

  • System members with xenogenitals who wouldn’t want to use the term Xenogenitalia alone, since the meaning has to do with only thinking of yourself as having them, and not actually having them.
  • Intersex/Fantasy intersex people in a system, where the body is non-intersex.
  • Mostly for either people who transition to have xenogenitals in system, or for people who were ‘born’ with atypical sex characteristics that don’t coincide with the body.
