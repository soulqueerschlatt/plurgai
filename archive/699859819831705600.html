Absgender is a gender modality in which the person feels their gender experience is in between, beyond or removed from cisgender / transgender dichotomy; being neither cis nor trans; or both cisn’t and trans’t.

This flag is specifically for System members who have a different past than the body.

Some examples:

  • Trans Women system members in D/AFAB bodies could decide to id as Abs instead of Trans/Cis.
  • Trans Men system members in D/AMAB bodies could decide to id as Abs instead of Trans/Cis
  • Cis Men system members in D/AFAB bodies could decide to id as Abs instead of Trans/Cis
  • Cis Women system members in D/AMAB bodies could decide to id as Abs instead of Trans/Cis
